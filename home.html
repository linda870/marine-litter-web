About Marine Litter Med Plus

Marine Litter Med Plus is a citizen science and environmental monitoring initiative focused on tracking and documenting the presence of marine litter in the Mediterranean Sea. The project enables volunteers, researchers, and coastal communities to collect standardized data on both macro and microplastic pollution, helping to better understand sources, distribution patterns, and environmental impact.

Our mission is to foster collaboration and data sharing among Mediterranean countries to support evidence-based policies and solutions for marine pollution reduction. By providing easy-to-use digital tools for litter collection journeys, the project ensures high-quality data collection and public awareness across the region.
